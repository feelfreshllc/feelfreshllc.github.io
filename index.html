Who Are We.

We moved from Albania to Waterbury 6 years ago with degrees in business marketing & nursing. So, what to do with that? Start a service company, of course! Our work ethics combined with our hospitality would be the cornerstone for the business that we have nurtured from the ground up. We saw a need in Connecticut for a local house cleaning company. Time is the most valuable thing we have and let's us give you back some of that precious time by helping you clean your home or business!

Our Goal.

Our goal is to help you feel fresh at your own home. Let US do the hard work for you. We understand just how busy life can be and how difficult it can be to stay on top of your cleaning chores. We can ensure that your home will always look neat, tidy, organized and, inviting, allowing you more time to focus on what is important to you and follow your dreams. Our team will be working hard, so you have a spotlessly clean home to return to.
